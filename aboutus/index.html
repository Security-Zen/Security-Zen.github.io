Who is Security Zen?

We are Salesforce Administrators, Developers, and Business Analysts like yourself.

We created Security Zen after many long hours of clicking boxes for complex security deployments. We thought, there has to be a better way! After much searching, we couldn't find anything that let us do the security comparisons and deployments that we wanted in an easy-to-view format that anyone could use.

So, we decided to build the tool we really wanted and named it for the peace and serenity it has given us: Security Zen!
